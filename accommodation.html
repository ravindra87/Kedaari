  • Camping and Trekking:

    For camping and trekking experiences, the proximity to natural wonders and trekking trails is crucial. Our camping sites and trekking routes are carefully selected to provide you with the best experiences. Here are a few examples:


    Chandrashila Trek:
    If you're embarking on the Chandrashila Trek, our camping sites are strategically located near Chopta. They offer breathtaking views of the Himalayas and are in close proximity to the trekking trail that leads to the summit of Chandrashila peak.

    Valley of Flowers Trek:
    For the Valley of Flowers Trek, our camping sites are located near the entrance of the national park. You can wake up to the sight of vibrant alpine flowers and easily access the trail that takes you through this natural paradise.
  • Transportation Options:

    We understand that reaching your accommodation from nearby towns or cities is an important consideration. Here are the transportation options available:

    Kedarnath and Guptkashi:
    Both Kedarnath and Guptkashi are well-connected by road. You can hire private taxis, take shared jeeps, or use public buses to reach these destinations from major nearby towns and cities like Rishikesh, Haridwar, and Dehradun. Our accommodations can provide assistance in arranging transportation for you.

    Camping and Trekking Sites
    The transportation options vary depending on the location. For camping and trekking sites, our team will provide detailed information about the nearest transportation hubs, such as railway stations or bus stops. We can also arrange transportation services or guide you on the best ways to reach these sites.

Interesting fact about the CHOPTA CHANNDRASHILA TREK

- Chopta Chandrashila trek reaches an altitude of 4,000 meters (13,000 feet) at the Chandrashila Summit.
- Tungnath Temple, located on the trek route, is the highest Shiva temple in the world.
- The trek offers stunning panoramic views of Himalayan peaks like Nanda Devi, Trishul, Kedarnath, and Chaukhamba.
- Deoriatal Lake, a beautiful lake surrounded by forests and mountains, is often included in the trek itinerary.

  • Brief Description About the Trek

    The Chopta Chandrashila trek is a mesmerizing journey that takes you through the pristine beauty of the Garhwal region in Uttarakhand, India. The trek begins from the picturesque village of Chopta, nestled at an altitude of 2,680 meters (8,790 feet). From here, you embark on a trail that winds its way through lush green meadows, dense forests, and panoramic mountain vistas.
    One of the highlights of the trek is the ancient Tungnath Temple, located at an elevation of 3,680 meters (12,073 feet). This revered temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and holds immense religious significance for devotees. As you ascend towards Tungnath, you'll be awestruck by the captivating views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks, including Nanda Devi, Trishul, Kedarnath, and Chaukhamba.
    Continuing on the trail, you reach the pinnacle of the trek - the Chandrashila Summit, standing tall at 4,000 meters (13,000 feet) above sea level. The final ascent to Chandrashila offers a thrilling and rewarding experience. As you reach the summit, you are greeted with a breathtaking panoramic view of the majestic snow-capped Himalayas, stretching as far as the eye can see. The sight of the golden sunrise casting its first rays on the peaks is truly awe-inspiring and leaves an indelible impression on your soul.
    Throughout the trek, you are treated to a kaleidoscope of natural beauty. The route passes through enchanting forests of rhododendron, oak, and deodar, offering a refreshing respite from the world below. The diverse flora and fauna of the region add to the allure, with chances to spot a variety of bird species and wildlife along the way.
    The Chopta Chandrashila trek is a moderate-level trek that can be undertaken by both seasoned trekkers and beginners with reasonable fitness levels. It is recommended to undertake the trek between the months of April to November when the weather is pleasant and the trails are accessible. The trek offers a perfect blend of adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty, making it a favorite among nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and spiritual seekers.
    Embark on the Chopta Chandrashila trek, immerse yourself in the serenity of the mountains, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

  • DETAILED ITINERARY


    Day 1 - Reach Chopta | Check in to Camps / Hotel

    - We'll be reaching Chopta by the Afternoon.
    - Upon reaching, we'll check into our campsite and feshen up. After that, evening snacks will be served.
    - Rest of the day is at leisure & relax in the lap of nature.
    - Pass through Srinagar and Rudraprayag towns.
    - Dinner will be served in the evening followed by an overnight stay at Chopta Campsite.

  • Day 2 - Trek 5KM from Chopta to Tungnath Chandrashila peak.

    - We'll have an early breakfast as today will be the day we trek mainly.
    - Afterwards, we'll start to trek up from Chopta - the starting point of the trek.
    - The first checkpoint will be covered upon reaching Tungnath Temple. The second checkpoint for the day will be Chandrashila Top.
    - Lastly, we'll trek down 5KM to Chopta once again and be back in our campsite.
    - Duration: 5-6 hours
    - Trek type: Easy-moderate. Gradual ascent throughout on a well-defined trail.
    - Enjoy the scenic beauty and surroundings during the trek.
    - Dinner will be served followed by an overnight stay in Chopta Campsite.

  • Day 3 - Chopta to Sari and trek 2.3KM to Deoriatal | Back to Delhi

    - We'll start with an early breakfast followed by checking out of the campsite.
    - Afterwards, we'll drive to Sari Village - the starting point of Deoriatal Trek. Upon reaching, we'll start for the 2.3KM Deoriatal Trek and upon completing the trek, be back again at the Sari Village.
    - Lastly, we'll board again for Delhi for an overnight journey by the Afternoon.
    - We'll reach Delhi Early Morning. The group will disassemble here and leaves with a set of happy memories, and more.

  • Brief Description About the Trek

    The Dayara Bugyal Trek is a popular trekking route located in the Indian state of Uttarakhand, specifically in the Uttarkashi district. "Bugyal" in the local language means "meadow," and Dayara Bugyal is known for its vast alpine meadows spread across an elevation of approximately 10,000 feet (3,048 meters) to 12,000 feet (3,657 meters).
    The trek offers breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, lush green meadows, dense forests, and a variety of flora and fauna. It is a moderate-level trek, suitable for both beginners and experienced trekkers. The total trek distance is around 9 to 12 kilometers, depending on the starting point, and can be completed in about 4 to 5 days.
    The trek usually starts from the village of Barsu, which serves as the base camp. From Barsu, trekkers begin their ascent through a scenic trail that passes through dense oak and rhododendron forests. As they climb higher, the landscape opens up to vast meadows, offering panoramic views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks, including Bandarpoonch and Draupadi Ka Danda.
    Camping at Dayara Bugyal is a unique experience, as trekkers get to spend nights under the starry sky in the midst of nature's beauty. The meadows are adorned with colorful flowers during the summer months, making it a picturesque sight.
    The trek also provides opportunities for adventure enthusiasts to indulge in activities like snowboarding and skiing during the winter season when the meadows are covered in snow.
    Throughout the trek, trekkers can witness the local Garhwali culture and interact with the friendly villagers of Barsu. The route is relatively well-marked, and trekkers are accompanied by experienced guides and support staff.
    Overall, the Dayara Bugyal Trek offers a captivating journey through pristine natural beauty, making it a popular choice among nature lovers, adventure seekers, and photography enthusiasts.

  • DETAILED ITINERARY


    DAY 1 DEHRADUN TO BARSU - 186km And 6-7 hour

    - We'll be reaching BARSU by the Afternoon.
    - Roll call & Pick up from Dehradun at 7 AM.
    - Rest of the day is at leisure & relax in the lap of nature.
    - Dinner will be served in the evening followed by an overnight stay at BARSU Campsite.

  • Day 2 - Trek 5KM from BARSU TO BARNALA TAL.

    - We'll have an early breakfast as today will be the day we trek mainly.
    - Afterwards, we'll start to trek up from BARSU - the starting point of the trek.
    - 5 Km hike to Barnala Tal meadow passing through the oak forest
    - Arrival at the campsite, hot lunch at the campsite.
    - Trek type: Easy-moderate. Gradual ascent throughout on a well-defined trail.
    - Enjoy the scenic beauty and surroundings during the trek.
    - Dinner will be served followed by an overnight stay in Campsite.

  • Day 3 - BARNALA TAL TO DAYARA BUGYAL SUMMIT & BACK

    - We'll start with an early breakfast followed by checking out of the campsite.
    - Wake up early morning & have breakfast.
    - Trek to Dayara Bugyal & summit thereafter for 4.5 Kms.
    - Rest and packed lunch at the Dayara Viewpoint.
    - Descend back to Barnala Tal Campsite from Dayara Top.
    - Arrival at the campsite Dinner at the campsite.

  • Day 4: BARNALA TAL TO BARSU & BACK TO DEHRADUN

    - Early morning breakfast
    - Trek back to Barsu.
    - Departure to Dehradun. 186 kilometers (125 miles).
    - As you traverse the winding roads, relish the scenic beauty of Uttarakhand's landscapes and the majestic Himalayas.
    - Take advantage of the journey to pause at scenic viewpoints, capturing the breathtaking views and creating lasting memories.
    - As the day draws to a close, find accommodation in Dehradun and rest, relishing the memories of your journey from Basu.
